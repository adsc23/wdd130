Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this page is to stablish an online presence for our business so we can connect easily with our customers and help new customers to find our business. Our main goal is to capture the interest of people that wants to learn more about rafting.

Audience

Our website is designed to capture the interest of bold individuals who seek outdoor experiences. These indivuals typically range from 18 to 40 years old, with active lives and interests in exploring nature. We will target seasoned rafters looking for challenging rapids and people eager to try something new. Visitors from our site will most likely be looking to find detailed information about our rafting trips, difficulty levels from those trips, safety measures, equipment provided, and the stunning locations we operate in. They would also want to know about booking procedures, availability, and any special offers.
