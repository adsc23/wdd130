Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this page is to stablish an online presence for our business so we can connect easily with our customers and help new customers to find our business. Our main goal is to capture the interest of people that wants to learn more about rafting.

Audience

Our website is designed to capture the interest of bold individuals who seek outdoor experiences and people looking for new experiences. To find those individuals we considered the following:

  1. Who are the target customers? Our target customers will be people interested in natural experiences, with bold actitude, and age averaging the 18 to 45 years old.
  2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? With our website we will provide them information about the best places to do Rafting in our area, we will provide special and exclusive packages so they can get out the best experience from our area. We will also provide the best quality in equipment for rafting so they can safely enjoy their experience.
  3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? We expect that most of our visitors will be using a computer (for adults averaging +30 years), however, we will make sure our website will be very responsive in mobile devices. Our long term goal will be also to implement a app version of our website so we can focus on selling our packages and equipment and get to young people.
